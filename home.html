About Us

YECS is a Non Govermantal Organization(NGO) whose interest is to promotes and help people in need, the organization is based and located in FHA Lugbe, Police Sign Board, Abuja Nigeria.

  • Culture of collaboration – Working with strategic partnership for a better tomorrow
  • Dedication to diversity and integrity – We are an inclusive entity working with ethics and fairness
  • Passion – This is the core of our organization knowing that there no end to learning so we strive to improve and innovate

Our mission and vision is to grow to be an organization with global standards providing empowerment initiatives.
